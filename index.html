This is how I initially set up my website, it means that especially on a wide screen, the content is scaled in the center with a max width, and large white margins on left and right. Ideally I would not like to change this div with the canvas id (as it applies to every page on my website), unless you can convince me that it would be better to do so. The first three non-text sections on this page are what I call "panels with two images". I use them to put two images next to eachother, and when making the page width smaller, like on mobile, there will be a point where they go under each other instead of next to eachother.
